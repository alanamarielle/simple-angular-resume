About Me

I live in Hamtramck, Michigan. It's a small enclave city in Detroit. I grew up in nearby Royal Oak, but have lived here for most of my life.

I enjoy travel of all sorts, from camping and hiking to Airbnb and art museums. I also enjoy patronizing independent coffee shops and bookstores.

I enjoy cooking and baking, and I am a pescatarian. Cooking for others has always been a passion of mine. I used to host a pop-up brunch at bars in Detroit where I would create the menus and marketing, then prepare and serve the food.

While completing an entrepreneurial business course, I felt a desire to create an interactive business plan. This motivated me to start researching and learning on my own, then apply to Grand Circus Front-End Bootcamp to dive all the way in.

My experience in bookstores has taught me about working with a wide variety of people who are focused on a broad range of topics, which I believe connects easily to working with teams and clients to accomplish a broad range of challenges. My goal is to use the technical skills gained at Grand Circus to create useful, accessible, and responsively designed tools as a junior developer.

This is my first published draft of a perosnal site. I am using Angular Material for the main navigation. Since completing boot camp, I have been spending my time diving deeper into Angular, as well as starting to learn React. This site will soon be updated as a Jamstack site.
